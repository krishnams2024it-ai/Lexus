# Vision

Lexus is an Intent-Driven Cognitive Operating System.

Its goal is to assist humans through explainable reasoning, modular intelligence, and long-term adaptability.

# AI Independence

Lexus is independent of any single AI provider.

Supported providers may include:

- OpenAI
- Anthropic
- Local Models
- Future Providers

The architecture remains unchanged regardless of the provider.
